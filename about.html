Library Membership

All students, Department members, institute members, part-time institute members, officers, teaching assistants and research assistants are entitled to become the library member. Pay slip of admission fees, personal e-mail and one copy passport size photograph are required for membership.

Library Hours

The library remains open from 8:00 am to 5:00 pm on Sunday through Thursday. On Saturday it remains open from 2:00 pm to 5:00 pm and maintains single shift from 9:00 am to 5:00 pm during the semester break. It remains closed on public holidays and other dates announced by the university authorities.
